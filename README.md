# Society of One

- Society predisposes people in it to certain way, People are susceptible to the predispositions and assumptions the social systems create.

---

_Society of One_ enphasises three crucial aspects of this new model for the future of human society: autonomy, modularity, and integrity.

First, empowering each _one_ as the architect of their own social environment. People are the creators and the consumers of society at the same time. Once the two roles are out of balance, society corrupts into a game of discriminating who builds and who's bound. Society is healthier when we contribute closer to where we are. Of course, it's more difficult to create order than to rely on it. That's why the roles often divide people and centralise power to capable few. A better framework must differentiate individuals and enable each of them to connect as they see fit based on interdependence. 

Second, social progress in experimental _units_ within manageable scopes and timeframes, like projects. Society is a catch-all term for any human interaction in each passing moment. As much as it sounds abstract, it's difficult to organise and improve such a concept systematically. Dutch explorers invented corporation to bundle up commercial ventures and make them available for individuals. We can imagine a modular way to frame society, instead of pretending it's one big swamp we all signed up. The attraction is that people can exemplify possibilities or fail safely in this sandbox. The empowered individuals can choose to initiate, collaborate, or leave the social units.

Third, human society truly _unified_ in a flexible network. History of humanity flows one direction and one direction only: wholeness. Yet simple assimilation was proved to falls short. Monolithic part of society should remain as a thin layer that supports constellations of diversity. Better consolidation comes from better separation discussed above. The ephemeral nature of social units can spin up stable and persistent cycles where people can dwell. The cosmopolitan vision is possible when society wakes up to its soft evolution and dissolves national borders. 

In summary, _Society of One_ means free individuals creating value together in experimental social units enabled by a cosmopolitan network.
